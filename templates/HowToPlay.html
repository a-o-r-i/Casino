How to play

Coinflip

1v1

Create a session by choosing Heads or Tails and setting a bet. The player who joins automatically takes the other side.

When both sides are filled, the coin flips once. If it lands on your side, you win the pot.

Choices
Heads or Tails
Result
One flip

Dice

2 modes

Classic

Pick Low or High and set a bet. Low wins on 1 to 3. High wins on 4 to 6. One die is rolled, and the matching side wins the pot.

First To

Choose FT1, FT3, or FT5. Each round gives one point to the higher roll. Ties reroll until someone wins the round.

With Roll 2 dice per player enabled, both players roll two dice each round and the higher combined total takes the point.

Blackjack

Table

Create or join a table, claim a seat, then place a main bet before the round starts.

Try to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Number cards keep their value, face cards count as 10, and aces count as 1 or 11.

Hit
Take another card.
Stand
Keep your hand and let play continue.
Double
Double your bet, take one card, then stand.
Split
When your first two cards match in value, split them into two hands with a matching bet.

Optional side bets include Perfect Pairs and 21+3. Insurance may appear when the dealer shows an ace.
